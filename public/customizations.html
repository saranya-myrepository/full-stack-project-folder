Customizations

Note to our customers:

At Stitch & Fetch, we take pride in crafting bags that are uniquely tailored to your preferences. Please note that our customization services are exclusively available for bags manufactured by our own. Imported or third-party bags are not eligible for customizations. Once your customization request is confirmed, our skilled artisans will carefully craft your bag, and the process will take approximately two weeks. Thank you for understanding, and we look forward to creating a bag that's as unique as you!
